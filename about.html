About me

Hi there, my name is Jacob Lewandowski and I am a self-taught full-stack software engineer. My journey with programming began as a hobby at first, however with each new piece of knowledge that I gained on this subject I developed an ever-growing passion for this field of work. Because of that, I have decided that I want to pursue coding as a career path.


There is nothing as satisfying as finding a solution to a complex problem or understanding how something works down to the nitty-gritty to me. As an engineer, but also an artistic soul I consistently try to combine my creativity and logical thinking to create beautiful and free-flowing applications, while integrating high-quality, readable, and maintainable code working in the background.


I am the type of person that wakes up every morning with the drive, and discipline to grow my knowledge, expertise, and skillset, so that I can go to sleep at the end of the day with the peace of mind that I improved compared to yesterday. That being said I believe myself to be a well-organized, self-motivated, and quite nerdy (huge J.R.R. Tolkien fan) person, that refuses to run away when faced with any challenge.


"Little by little, one travels far."

-J.R.R. Tolkien
