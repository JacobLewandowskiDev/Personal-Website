About me

Hi there, my name is Jacob Lewandowski and I am a graduate of the Wroclaw University of Science and Technology in Poland.with the title of an HVAC & MEP Engineer. What started as a hobby at first, quickly evolved into a deep passion and a pursuit for mastery in the art of programming. Thanks to the guidance and moral support of my friends I became a self-taught full stack software developer.


As a developer, I consistantly try to combine my artistic and philosophical ideas with the urge to solve problems; creating beautiful and free-flowing applications, intergratting high quality, readable and maintainable code working in the background.


I am the type of person that at the end of the day wants to go to sleep knowing that I improved compared to yesterday. Because I believe myself to be very disciplined, well organized, knowledge-hungry, and quite nerdy, I am not someone who runs away from a challenge. I love learning new things and finding out how stuff works; down to the nitty-gritty.
