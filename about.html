About me

My name is Jacob Lewandowski and I am a graduate of the Wroclaw University of Science and Technology in Poland with a master's engineering degree in environmental engineering. I have searched far and wide for what I wanted to do in life career-wise since the age of 16. I've worked many jobs and tried a plethora of activities just to find something that would be both challenging enough to not get bored of it, and exciting enough so that I look forward every day to learning more about it.


Thanks to the guidance and help from great friends, I was introduced to the world of programming for the first time. Since then, I felt a deep passion for the art of combining one's creativity and problem-solving skills, that this profession requires to create astonishing applications for both myself and other users. Thus my journey with learning to code began and continues to this day.


As for today, I am currently searching for the right Employer to hire me. A workspace in which I would be able to grow both professionally by improving my skills, and pushing me to become the very best version of myself would be considered a perfect one for me.


I have a strong passion for programming and writing clean, readable, and maintainable code, I come with good organizational skills and am very keen on obtaining new knowledge every day be that learning new technologies, techniques, and other skills that might be required by Your company in a fairly short amount of time. Because I enjoy STEM field-related challenges I like tackling complex problems in the search for a solution - which in my opinion is one of the most important traits any programmer should have.


If you are interested in me and my skills I would be delighted to schedule a meeting with you. Feel free to contact me.
