About me

Hi there, my name is Jacob Lewandowski and I am a graduate of the Wroclaw University of Science and Technology in Poland. I have searched far and wide for what I wanted to do in life career-wise since the age of 16. I've worked a dozen jobs and tried a plethora of activities just to find that special something that I would gravitate towards


Thanks to a group of great friends of mine, I was introduced to the world of programming for the first time back in the year 2020. My journey as a programmer started by learning Java as a hobby. Since then, I felt a deep passion for the art of combining one's creativity and problem-solving skills, which this profession requires to create astonishing applications.


I have been learning to code in my free time between classes in college. After my graduation in July of 2021, I've decided to dive deep into the world of programming. Being a self-taught engineer is definitely a great test to one's discipline, willpower, and determination to reach a desired goal. Like every other programmer out there I have had my share of ups and downs on this path, but there is no better feeling than the feeling of accomplishment you get when you finally figure out the solution to your coding problem.


I am a type of person that wants to constantly be growing and become a better version of myself, and I think that is the exact reason why I fell in love with coding. The challenges I face today might become trivial tomorrow or in a week's time, but I will always keep on moving forward.


"Our very survival depends on our ability to stay awake, to adjust to new ideas, to remain vigilant and to face the challenge of change." -Martin Luther King Jr.
